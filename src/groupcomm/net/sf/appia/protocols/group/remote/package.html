Gives to non-members of a group the possibility of knowing the membership of a group.

The members of the group must have the {@link net.sf.appia.protocols.group.remote.RemoteGossipOutLayer} in a channel, replacing the tipical {@link net.sf.appia.protocols.group.heal.GossipOutLayer}. Also the {@link net.sf.appia.protocols.group.remote.RemoteViewLayer} must be in a channel of the application interested in knowing the membership.

In order to take advantage of this functionality a gossip server must be running for the group.

The requests are sent trough a {@link net.sf.appia.protocols.group.remote.RemoteViewEvent}
